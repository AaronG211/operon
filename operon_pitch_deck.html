Operon

Your AI Business Consultant for Running a Smarter, More Profitable Restaurant.

The Problem:
Data Overload, Insight Starved

  • Most restaurant owners have scattered data (reviews, POS systems, spreadsheets, costs).
  • They lack the time, integrated systems, or analytical expertise to interpret this data into actionable improvements.
  • Result: Decisions are made on gut feeling, leaving profit margins squeezed and operational issues unresolved.

The Solution:
Operon's Always-On AI

  • An AI consultant that automatically analyzes business performance across all metrics.
  • Identifies root causes of problems (e.g., specific high-food-cost items, delivery app dependence).
  • Translates raw data into specific, actionable next steps in plain English.
  • Acts like a dedicated data analyst at a fraction of the cost.

Target Audience

  • Primary Focus: Independent restaurant owners and small local groups.
  • Size: 1 to 5 locations.
  • Pain Points: Struggling with tight profit margins and disjointed operations.
  • Readiness: Already tracks some data but frustrated by lack of actionable summaries.

How It Works (Simplicity First)

  • Step 1: Onboarding & Data Input
    • Simple UI to input revenue, food costs, menu items, and copy/paste reviews.
    • No complex POS integrations required for Day 1 MVP.
  • Step 2: AI Health Check Generation
    • Our engine scans the data to check Revenue, Margin, & Menu Health.

Key Feature:
Actionable Recommendations

  • Operon cuts through the noise.
  • It provides a prioritized list of specific recommendations based on uploaded numbers.
  • Example: "Raise the price of the Pad Thai by $1.50 - demand is high but margin is weak."
  • Each recommendation includes an expected impact and effort level indicator.

Key Feature:
AI Consultant Chat

  • Context-Aware AI: The chat acts as an extension of your specific business report.
  • Users can ask follow-up questions:
    • "Why did my profit drop this week?"
    • "Which menu items should I promote on Instagram today?"
  • Answers are generated based exclusively on the restaurant's uploaded data and context.

Key Feature:
Weekly Summary

  • A proactive pulse check for the restaurant owner.
  • Highlights the overall business trend compared to the last period.
  • Identifies the single biggest issue and largest opportunity for that specific week.
  • Delivers 3 quick-win recommended actions to keep growth on track.

Seamless User Experience

  • Minimal Friction: Operon accepts lightweight CSV uploads or manual entry.
  • Instant Gratification: Instantly translates raw numbers into easy-to-read, aesthetically pleasing dashboards.
  • No confusing spreadsheets—just visual, executive-level summaries of business health.

The Vision

Transforming restaurant intelligence from an enterprise luxury into an accessible, profit-driving necessity for every independent owner.
